Braised Pork Belly Recipe

Ingredients

  • 5 slices of ginger
  • 2 green onions (cut into 3 inch slices)
  • 1/2 cup light soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup dark soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup Shaoxing wine
  • 2 pound slab of pork belly

Steps

  1. Cut pork belly into 1 inch slices
  2. Turn Instant Pot on sauté setting, then add oil and ginger. Caramelize for 30 seconds. Then add green onions and cook for another 30 seconds. Add the soy sauces, sugar, salt, water, and Shaoxing wine
  3. Close the lid of the Instant pot with the vent closed. Cook for 5 minutes on the manual setting
  4. Safely release pressure and lower pork belly slices into the Instant Pot
  5. Cook the pork belly for 17 minutes on the soup/broth setting. Release the pressure and flip pork belly and cook for another 5 minutes on the same setting
  6. Remove pork belly from Instant Pot and into smaller pieces as desired
  7. Cook the sauce on the sauté setting until reduced
  8. Serve over rice and green veggie of choice (e.g. bok choy) with sauce spooned over. Top with green onions and sesame seeds if desired
